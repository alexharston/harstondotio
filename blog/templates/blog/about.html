About

I'm Alex, a first-year PhD Student in the Brain and Behaviour Lab at Imperial College London. I previously did Systems Neuroscience in Edinburgh, working in volitional motor control in mice. I've gone more computational (and human-focused) recently, and currently work in the area of action intention prediction, using deep convolutional neural networks, LSTMs and autoregressive models for multivariate time-series analysis. My other interests include information security, web development, design, and learning to make the perfect espresso.
