About

I'm Alex Harston, a first-year PhD Student in the Brain and Behaviour Lab at Imperial College London. I previously did Systems Neuroscience in Edinburgh, working in volitional motor control in mice. I currently work in the area of action intention prediction, using neural networks and other machine learning methods for multivariate time-series modelling. My interests include infosec, web development, espresso and data visualisation.
