about me

Hi, I'm Alex. I'm 24, and currently in the first year of my PhD at Imperial College London. I work in the Faisal Lab, and broadly work on human-in-the-loop machine learning, as it pertains to neuroscience. I previously did Systems Neuroscience in Edinburgh, working on understanding volitional motor control in mice. Nowadays I work in the area of human action intention prediction, using a variety of machine learning methods for multivariate time-series analysis.
